Shree Gayatri Industries was incepted in 2007, in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. Beyond our core expertise in Roll Forming Machines, we are a leading supplier of high-quality Rolling Shutter Motors and IoT Smart Automation accessories.

In order to fully satisfy our clients, we offer motors ranging from Single Phase setups for commercial shops to heavy-duty Three Phase systems for large industrial bays.

Under the strategic direction of Mr. Ketan Dodiya (CEO & Proprietor), we have greatly expanded our clientele within the nation, maintaining a reputation for reliability, precision, and state-of-the-art technological integrations.
